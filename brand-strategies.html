Distinctive Brand Identities & Packaging Designs { using a customer centric approach }

STRATEGIC BRAND CREATION & DEVELOPMENT AGENCY

Developing customer-centric Brand Strategies, creating Brand Identities and crafting Packaging Designs that connect with the customers values and aspirations is what we specialise in.

Branding is so much more than creating a logo and a businesses visual identity. Branding is also something that isn’t easily described. Lets start by imagining that we are the customer. There’s no denying, that as customers we have an endless supply of choices, whether those choices are; Hotels, Airlines, Cars or even a service that we require. And since the 1950’s our main drivers behind the brands that we purchase, have been largely based on ‘Price #1’ and ‘Availability #2’. So now that the Internet has become such an entrenched part of our purchasing and researching behaviour, how has it affected our ‘Price’ and ‘Availability’ expectations? We all know the answer. If a business states a ‘price-point’, then we use the Internet to find a better price-point. If an item is out-of-stock, we search somewhere else to find one that’s in-stock. So competing on ‘Price’ and ‘Availability’ if you’re a smaller player in the market is like swimming against the tide. This is where the ‘value’ of having or creating a ‘brand’ steps in. Brands rely on a different set of

customer drivers. ‘Ease of Use #3’ and ‘Quality #4’. Although ‘Price’ is always relevant, it’s not the ‘main driver’ for those customers looking for a ‘Quality’ product or service and also one that offers ‘Ease of use or engagement’. Therefore the communication style of businesses trying to compete on ‘Quality’ and ‘Engagement’ need to get it exactly right. Creating an authentic brand personality simply ‘sets the stage’, to help customers imagine, what it will be like to engage with you as a business. Does your brand convey your ‘Quality’ and ‘Engagement style’ without the customer having to read a single word? Does your Brand communicate an unspoken set of values, that your ideal customers can relate to? Creating authentic brands that connect with what the customer values, and establishing a promise of what it would be like to engage with your business is a brand that helps you to stand-out as potentially ideal to your customers’ needs.
